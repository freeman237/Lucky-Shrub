Lucky Shrub: Your Garden Oasis Awaits

Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm based in Tucson, Arizona. Specializing in garden design, creation, maintenance, and landscaping, the company also operates a thriving plant nursery that offers a wide selection of indoor and outdoor plants. With their expertise and one-stop-shop approach, Lucky Shrub helps clients transform any space into a verdant, beautiful oasis

Garden Design and Creation

At the heart of Lucky Shrub's services lies their expert garden design and creation capabilities. Led by Jason, the company's talented garden architect, the team crafts personalized designs that bring clients' visions to life. From initial concept to final installation, they meticulously plan and execute every detail to ensure a harmonious and functional outdoor space.

Maintenance and Landscaping

Maintaining the health and beauty of a garden is crucial, and Lucky Shrub offers comprehensive maintenance and landscaping services. Their skilled team of landscapers works diligently to keep gardens thriving through regular upkeep, pruning, and care. Whether it's a sprawling outdoor retreat or a meticulously manicured urban landscape, Lucky Shrub ensures your green space remains a lush, inviting oasis

Nursery and Plant Selection

Complementing their design and landscaping expertise, Lucky Shrub operates a well-stocked plant nursery. Overseen by Maria, the nursery offers a diverse selection of indoor and outdoor plants, catering to a wide range of preferences and needs. Clients can conveniently find the perfect plants to complement their garden designs or to simply add a touch of greenery to their living spaces
